Our Brand Story

IMO Airconditioning Services Pvt. Ltd. was established in October 1996 by Founder Director Mr. Narayan Lakshmanan. We got the ISO certification 9001:2008 from 2008-2017 from Bureau Veritas. We are an authorized sales & service dealer for leading HVAC brands.

Our business division is located in Chembur-E, Mumbai with an integrated facility housing all processes under one roof. We have a 1000 sq.ft. sales office and a 1000 sq.ft. service station & godown fully equipped with tools and tackles.

Our team consists of over 69 personnel including sales officers, office staff, service managers, ITI certified service mechanics, and erection managers/supervisors who strive to achieve our mission of customer satisfaction.
